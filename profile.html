STRAY KIDS

Stray Kids (abbreviated as SKZ) is a Korean boy group debuted on March 25, 2018 under JYP Entertaiment. They were formed through competition reality show of the same name on 2017. Stray Kids composed of eight members: Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N. Originally formed as nine, but member Woojin left due personal reason on October 2019. Bang Chan (the leader) was picked each member of the group himself.

They are attracting a lot of attention as a group with extraordinary talent, such as the members themselves involved in writing, composing, and producing their own music. 3RACHA (Stray Kids sub-unit consist of: Bang Chan, Changbin, and Han) credited as writing and producing the majority of Stray Kidsâ€™ discography alongside JYP producers. Based on STAYANALITICS data by July 4, 2022, Stray Kids have been participate on 100% writing, 93% composing, and 42.5% arranging in their albums production (from a total 101 songs on released albums only).
